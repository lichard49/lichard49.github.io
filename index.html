Research Overview

My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, embedded systems, and human-computer interaction, materializing in the form of wearable and ubiquitous computers. I work with James Fogarty and Shwetak Patel (in the Ubicomp Lab) among many other amazing collaborators across multiple departments at the University of Washington on a variety of topics ranging from mobile health applications to novel user interfaces. I publish this work at academic venues such as the Interactive, Mobile, Wearable and Ubiquitous Technologies (IMWUT) journal and at the Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI); see below for a complete list of publications.
