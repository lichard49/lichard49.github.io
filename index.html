Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, human computer interaction, and embedded systems, materializing in the form of wearable and ubiquitous computers. I work in the Ubicomp Group and the Contextual Computing Group at Georgia Tech on a variety of topics ranging from mobile health applications to novel user interfaces.
