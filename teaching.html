UW CSE/ECE 475: Embedded Systems Capstone

Instructor, Autumn 2024

Embedded systems capstone is a project-based course for senior-level undergraduate students and masters students in electrical engineering, computer engineering, and computer science. I prepared and taught lectures on advanced embedded systems topics including PCB design, wireless communication, and on-device machine learning. In addition to lectures, I also ran lab sessions and guided 9 team projects from ideation to final presentation.

UW CSE 590U: Ubiquitous Computing Seminar

Instructor, Autumn 2021 - Present

Ubiquitous computing seminar is a forum for researchers interested in ubiquitous computing, human-computer interaction, and mobile systems to discuss the seminal and latest work in these fields. I organized weekly sessions for students and guests to present on their own work and work from the literature.
