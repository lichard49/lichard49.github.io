Tactile Teacher: Enhancing Traditional Piano Lessons

Piano lessons traditionally consist of students watching and listening to the teacher demonstrate a passage of music, and then attempting to mimic it. Tactile Teacher allows teachers to also share the tactile sensation of tapping the piano keys to help students better prepare for their own attempt.
